LEAP @ ALU

LEAP: Learning Efficacy Advancement Programme is a student-led academic peer mentorship programme that is coordinated by the Learning Support Associate at African Leadership University Campuses. LEAP is set up with the sole aim of providing academic advancement to all students irrespective of their academic progress. Student at ALU should not feel alone as they negotiate and develop pathways for their academic leadership journey, but should learn and share with each other as they embrace the myriad opportunities provided by student support systems such as LEAP, Language Support Programme (LSP), Student Life and Career Development.

Vision Statement
The vision of the Student Academic Mentoring Program is to enhance the quality of academic experience and improve the progress and retention of the student at ALU. This will thereby contribute to the cumulative academic success of the student body.
​ Mission
We strive to strengthen comprehensive learning, promote student academic advancement by providing academic support services such as on-campus mentorship, individualised tutoring, and the Writing centre services to ALU students. This is accomplished through the promotion and development of an inclusive learning and mentorship environment where students can master the skills needed to become successful lifelong learners.

Academic Mentoring @LEAP

Mentoring in general means that a person who is more knowledgeable, skilled and trained offers to help and support others in the form of training or advice. In this case, Academic Mentoring means that this situation applies to your education and career goals during your stay here as an ALU student. A student who has the necessary knowledge and skills can help and support you to improve your educational outputs and helps you to define your academic and non-academic goals clearly.

How can Academic Mentoring help me?

Academic Mentoring will help you as a student to reach your full potential, in a way that your academic progress is monitored, analysed and improved. This is based on your personal, educational and career goals and aims. We can help you to reach your set goals.

Who are Academic Mentoring Services for?

Academic Mentoring Services are designed for every ALU student currently enrolled or ALU student(s) that is currently on academic leave. The services are available to suit all levels, interests and learning needs of every ALU student.

How is Academic Peer to Peer mentorship​ work?

Peer to peer mentorship involves student(s) that have been recruited and trained by LEAP as peer mentors and students seeking academic advancement. The LEAP team screens the mentees after filling out the LEAP forms to clarify their needs during the mentorship process.
